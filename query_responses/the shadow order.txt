
The Shadow Order is a puppet-master organization that has slowly been infilitrating the world's governments over the
last 50 years. Although the extent of their true power is unknown, there is little doubt that they are the single
largest threat to freedom on Earth. The leader of The Shadow Order is an unknown and hotly debated topic. Many believe
The Shadow Order does not have one leader, but many. Regardless, The Resistance has made the identification of their
leader priority one.
